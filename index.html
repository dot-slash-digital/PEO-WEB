Nancy Edwards, owner and operator of Personal Eyes, began her career in the optical industry in 1979 with Benson Optical. Nancy became licensed and served as the area manager for the Las Vegas/Carson City area.
In 1989, Nancy opened Personal Eyes Opticians, an owner-operated business located in the Summerlin area of Las Vegas.
Today's eyewear is extremely deliberate, defining and fun! Eyewear can artistically accessorize and express your face even before you speak. Whether you are looking for unique fashion on a budget or the finest in designer eyewear, you always have many choices at Personal Eyes!
Never underestimate the power of what you wear, especially what is framing your face! Your face deserves our best... Come SEE the difference!
